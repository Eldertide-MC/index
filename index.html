About the Server

Eldertide is a Minecraft survival server where you can explore custom worlds, gather resources, and build your own adventure. Join a friendly community and survive together through challenging environments, exciting quests, and epic battles.

Features

Enjoy custom-built worlds, thrilling quests, and a vibrant community. Eldertide offers regular events, a balanced survival mode, and a place to create and collaborate with fellow players.

Community

Join a welcoming and supportive community at Eldertide. Whether you're building, exploring, or competing, our players share a passion for Minecraft. Together, we create unforgettable memories.

Server Rules

To keep Eldertide enjoyable for everyone, we ask all players to respect each other, avoid cheating, and refrain from griefing. Let’s keep it fun and fair for all!
